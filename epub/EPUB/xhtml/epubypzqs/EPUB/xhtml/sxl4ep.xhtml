We hope that this Pocketbook of Palliative Medicine has been helpful. Its aim is to contain concise and targetted guidelines for non-palliative care doctors (i.e. for hospital and primary care doctors) to help them care for patients in their last few months of life.

Originally written in 2014 as the Isle of Wight Palliative Medicine Guidelines by Dr Graham Grove and Dr Paul Howard in 2014, this is a 2022 updated for the Australian context by the Gold Coast palliative care team, including Dr Graham Grove. An online and up to date copy of the pocketbook can be found at www.palliativewiki.com

The authors greatly appreciate the comments and suggestions in the 2014 original from Dr Teena Silakong, Dr Mil Chan, Dr David Isaac, Dr Alaisdair Gove, Dr Jenny Collier, pharmacist Sandra Clawson, Dr David Grove and Dr Nicole Grove.

Feedback or suggestions are encouraged. Please contact Graham Grove on graham@palliativewiki.com to pass on any feedback.
