The injectable preparations of the following medicines can be given subcutaneously. Subcutaneous compatibilities can be found here.

Many are only licensed for IV or IM use and thus the ampoule will be labelled ‘for IV or IM use only’.

Many are not available via the PBS.

Note that syrups or solutions intended for oral use cannot be given by injection.
