Before starting opioids

  • An acurate assessment of the cause of the pain is important to guide treatment
  • Is there a treatment (e.g. radiotherapy) to target the cause specifically?
  • Has allied health input been considered (e.g. physiotherapy, occupational therapy, social work, psychology and spiritual care)?
