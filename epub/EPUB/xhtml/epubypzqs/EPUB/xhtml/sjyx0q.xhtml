• Relieve reversible causes (e.g. urinary retention with catheterization)
  • Benzodiazepines are ideal for anxiety and restlessness
  • Antipsychotics are ideal for hallucinations
  • In refractory terminal restlessness, phenobarbitone is an option 
First line agents and doses
Haloperidol 0.5-2.5mg hourly subcut PRN
Clonazepam 0.5-1mg hourly subcut PRN

(or Midazolam (non-PBS) 2.5-5mg hourly subcut PRN)
Second-line agent and dose
Levomepromazine (non-PBS, SAS Cat C) 12.5-25mg hourly subcut PRN
Seek advice if 2 or more doses are ineffective, or if benefit lasts less than 1 hour
