If constipation with overflow could be the cause -> treat the constipation

Is there a treatable cause ->

  • Medication side-effect (e.g. due to antibiotic) may require modification of medications
  • Infections (e.g. Clositridium difficile) may require antibiotics
  • Pancreatic insufficiency as suggested by steatorrhoea (e.g. due to pancreas cancer) requires Creon

For diarrhoea requiring symptomatic treatment ->

  • First-line:
    • Loperamide 2-4mg PRN initially
      (Consider a regular dose based on PRN requirements)
  • Second-line:
    • ​​​​​​​Hyoscine butylbromide (steamlined authority) (Buscopan) 60-120mg via CSCI over 24 hours

For refractory diarrhoea, octreotide (PBS restrictions) may be helpful.
