The New York Heart Association Classification is helpful in determining prognosis:

  • NYHA Class 4 with a hospital admission due to cardiac failure within the last 6-months --> 50% 1-year survival

Class

Symptoms

1

None

2

Mild dyspnoea on ordinary activities

3

Marked limitation in activity

4

Almost bed-bound due to symptoms

 

Special cases:

  • Hypotension with a raised creatinine is associated with a particularly poor prognosis
