The following sources give additional guidance and information on palliative illnesses, their treatment and specific medications:

Palliative Medicine Handbooks

  • The Oxford Handbook of Palliative Medicine by Max Watson, Rachel Campbell, Nadini Vallath, Stephen Ward and Jo Wells

Palliative Medicine Textbooks

  • The Oxford Textbook of Palliative Medicine edited by Nathan Cherny, Mari Fallon, Stein Kaasa, Russell Portenoy and David Currow

Palliative Care Medication Books

  • Palliative Care Formulary by Andrew Wilcock, Paul Howard and Sarah Charlesworth
  • The Syringe Driver: Continuous Subcutaneous Infusions in Palliative Care by Andrew Dickman and Jennifer Schneider
