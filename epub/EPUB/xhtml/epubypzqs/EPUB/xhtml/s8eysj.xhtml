The following sources give additional guidance and information on palliative illnesses, their treatment and specific medications:

  • Palliative Medicine Handbooks
    • The Oxford Handbook of Palliative Medicine by Max Watson, Caroline Lucas, Andrew Hoy and Jo Wells
  • Palliative Medicine Textbooks
    • The Oxford Textbook of Palliative Medicine edited by Geoffery Hanks, Nathan Cherny, Nicholas Christakis, Marie Fallon, Stein Kaasa and Russell Portenoy
  • Palliative Care Medication Books
    • Palliative Care Formulary by Robert Twycross and Andrew Wilcock
    • The Syringe Driver: Continuous Subcutaneous Infusions in Palliative Care by Andrew Dickman and Jennifer Schneider
  • Palliative Medicine Websites
    • www.caresearch.com.au
