• Are given to provide analgesia for breakthrough pain
  • For patients on long-acting opioids, a dose of one-sixth to one-tenth of the total daily dose of opioid is usually required as the PRN dose (e.g. a person taking twice daily 60 mg of slow-release oral morphine will probably require breakthrough doses of 20 mg immediate-release oral morphine 2-hourly PRN)
