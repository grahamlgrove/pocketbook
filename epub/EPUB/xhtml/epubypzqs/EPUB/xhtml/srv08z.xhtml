The Child-Pugh scoring system is helpful in determining prognosis:

  • Child’s A (Score < 7) cirrhosis --> 80% 1-year survival
  • Child’s B (Score 7-9) cirrhosis --> 60% 1-year survival
  • Child’s C (Score > 9) cirrhosis --> 40% 1 year survival
