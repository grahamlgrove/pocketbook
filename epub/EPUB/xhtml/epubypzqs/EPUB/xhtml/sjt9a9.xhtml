  • Benzodiazepines may help dyspnoea, especially when anxiety plays a role

For dyspnoea near the end of life:

Morphine 2.5 mg hourly subcut PRN near the end of life

(Adjust opioid dose if already opioid tolerant)
For dyspnoea related to anxiety:

Clonazepam 0.25 mg hourly subcut PRN  

Or midazolam (non-PBS) 2.5 mg hourly subcut PRN
