Need a palliative care nurse +/- doctor to assess an inpatient in Robina or Gold Coast Hospital?

Email or fax a referral form to gc_palcare_referral@health.qld.gov.au or 5668 6759

During business hours please also phone 5668 6047 at Robina Hospital or 5687 0000 at Gold Coast Hospital

If you wish to urgently speak to the palliative care registrar (or consultant) please call switch.

Need a palliative care nurse +/- doctor to assess a patient in the Gold Coast community?

For a new referral, email or fax the referral form to gc_palcare_referral@health.qld.gov.au or 5668 6759 (referral forms available via Medical Director or the Health Service Website or via calling 1300 763 218)

For a patient already known to the team, call 1300 763 218 and ask to speak to a community nurse.

Need to speak specifically to a Gold Coast palliative care doctor for urgent advice?

Call Robina Hospital (5668 6000) and ask to speak to the palliative care registrar (or consultant) on call.

Need a palliative medicine consult to assess a patient in the rural Darling Downs or South West Queensland

For immediate advice, Monday to Friday between 8am and 4pm, call 1300 618 486 and ask to speak to the palliative care doctor

If not urgent, email a referral to gcruralpallcare@health.qld.gov.au
