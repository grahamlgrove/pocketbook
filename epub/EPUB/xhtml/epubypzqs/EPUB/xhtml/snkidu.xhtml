Pain that remains severe despite simple analgesia, opioids and adjuvant agents may require specialist medications or procedural interventions. Seek specialist advice in these instances, but options may include:

  • Psychological support and counselling
  • Specialist medications (e.g. methadone or lidocaine)
  • Procedural interventions (e.g. coeliac plexus block and other nerve blocks via interventional radiology)
