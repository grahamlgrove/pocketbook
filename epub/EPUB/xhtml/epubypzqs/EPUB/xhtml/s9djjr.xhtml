Consider trialling hyoscine butylbromide (Buscopan) (PBS streamlined authority)

  • 20mg four times daily orally or subcut, regularly or PRN
  • Can be given by CSCI (60mg over 24 hours via CSCI; titrate to 120mg over 24 hours if necessary)

Other options include peppermint water and simethicone

If constipation is contributing -> reduce stimulant laxatives and increase softener laxatives

If bowel obstruction might be contributing -> investigate and treat this as appropriate
