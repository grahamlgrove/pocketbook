Non-pharmacological management may include:

  • Mild exercise programs and routines
  • Education and counselling -> Accepting fatigue as part of the illness + Modifying activities and goals appropriately
  • Aids and equipment for energy conservation

Pharmacological management:

  • Steroids improve energy and appetite in some patients
    • Trial dexamethasone for 1 week at 4mg (or 2mg) orally daily
    • If no improvement, cease dexamethasone
    • If significant improvement, continue dexamethasone. Use for only a few weeks and/or reduce dose to 2mg (or 1mg) daily to reduce risk of side effects

Caffeine 100mg tablets each morning

Specialist options include psychostimulants
