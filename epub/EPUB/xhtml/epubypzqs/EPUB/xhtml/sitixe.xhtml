Abbreviations are not used frequently in this book, but where they are used, they are consistent with ones in common usage (e.g. DVT stands for deep venous thrombosis). The following abbreviations and symbols are relatively frequent in this book:

Abbreviation

Stands For

CSCI

Continuous subcutaneous infusion

IV

Intravenous

IM

Intramuscular

PBS

Pharmaceuticals Benefit Scheme

PRN

As required

SAS

Special Access Scheme

Subcut

(Or SC)

Subcutaneous
(Note that SC is not an approved abbreviation but is used in this book to save space. It can be misunderstood as sublingual therefore always use “subcut” on prescriptions.)
