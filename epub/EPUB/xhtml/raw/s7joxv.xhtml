Question to answer: What is the bladder volume? (Does the patient have a very full bladder)

Example clinical scenario: A patient in the last few days of life is very restless. Could discomfort from urinary retention be contributing to the restlessness?

Ultrasound protocol:

  • Probe to use: 1-5 MHz
  • Patient position: Lying supine with abdomen and suprapubic region exposed
  • Place probe in suprapubic region and locate bladder using ultrasound rotating with the probe in the transverse plane
  • Measure bladder horizontally (x)
  • Rotate probe 90 degrees to the sagittal plane
  • Measure bladder horizontally (y)
  • Measure bladder vertically (z)
  • Multiply x by y by z and then by the constant 0.72 to determine the bladder volume
